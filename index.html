INTRODUCTION:

Superior Interior Design offers a variety of home and office related interior design services. Each project is unique and whether you desire a simple hourly design consultation or an on-going three year project, we look forward to any challenges the project may present. Design services are packaged according to your needs, so you have options to fit your timing and budget. We are here to create a functional and attractive environment that is beneficial to your quality of life.

PROJECT SECTION

SERVICES PROVIDED:

  • Packaged Design Services – Interior design services are packaged according to your needs -- whether it is updating a room with new flooring, paint, furniture, and window treatments -- or a complete renovation or new construction. After the initial consultation, Superior Interior Design will analyze your needs and recommend several design service packages from which to choose that coincide with your budget requirements and timing.
  • Purchasing Services - Part of the design process can include procurement. This service can be a separate or an integral part of a design package. Superior Interior Design has access to a wide array of products from wholesale vendors. Our prices are very competitive.
  • Shopping Services -- Shopping can be overwhelming or pleasurable. Superior Interior Design can help you make wise choices more quickly. We will work with retailers, design centers, wholesalers, and internet providers, depending on your needs.
